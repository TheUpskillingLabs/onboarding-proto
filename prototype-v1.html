Welcome to The Upskilling Labs

The world of work is changing quickly. AI is reshaping nearly every profession, but technology is only part of the story. The Upskilling Labs exists to help people learn, adapt, build, and contribute alongside others.

Our Philosophy

What Is The Upskilling Labs?

The Labs is not a course, bootcamp, or traditional workforce program. It is a civic learning institution where people practice using new tools, solving real problems, and strengthening their communities together.

What is an Upskiller?

"Everyone who joins The Upskilling Labs begins as an Upskiller. The only prerequisite is a willingness to grow. Whether you’re a student or CEO, public servant or entrepreneur, artist or engineer, retired or just getting started, your background matters less than your curiosity, your generosity, and your willingness to learn. If you’re willing to grow, you belong here."

The Stages of Practice

The Stages of Practice help us understand how capability develops. They do not rank people. They describe where someone is in a specific capability. A person might be an Expert facilitator and a Novice in AI workflows at the same time.
