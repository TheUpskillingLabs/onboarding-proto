About

A new way of working — not just AI

Upskilling isn't only about learning AI tools. AI creates a new way of working — one that rewards judgment, collaboration, and the ability to move from idea to output.

"AI has made answers cheap. It hasn't made judgment cheap."

What we offer

Take charge of your future of work

Learn alongside peers — working on real problems, building a real portfolio, getting real mentorship from practitioners in your city.

Learn

Hands-on Build Cycles and workshops led by practitioners

Ship

Real projects delivered to real users — not demos, not homework

Connect

Peers, mentors, and a growing alumni network

Belong

A civic community that grows with you

Where we started

Born in Washington, DC

We launched in fall 2025 at DC Public Library — a volunteer-powered community that became its own organization in January 2026.

300+

People through our programming and mentorship since launch.

Where we're going

Open, and expanding

We're building in the open — any library, any city, the same replicable playbook. Profiles, projects, and playbooks stay public so the whole community can build on them.

Open by default

No gatekeeping, no waiting. Anyone can learn from our work, build on it, and contribute back.

What upskilling means here

Not a curriculum. Real work.

We're not a course platform. Upskilling here means learning through real, collaborative work — building things, making decisions, iterating with peers.

As a mentor, you support that process — not teach a subject. You bring your judgment; participants bring their problems.

Where your answers go

The next few questions publish your public mentor profile — upskillers find you in the community directory, and project teams reach you through evidence-backed requests you choose to pick up. The Labs team sees new mentor profiles too and can make introductions. Live immediately — no application, no review queue.
